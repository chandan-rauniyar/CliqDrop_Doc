About CliqDrop Extension

What is CliqDrop?

CliqDrop is a lightweight and secure file-and-text sharing extension for Zoho Cliq. It enables users to instantly send data across devices and between people using a simple 6-digit access code, without requiring login, authentication, or organization restrictions. Whether you want to move data between your own devices or share content with colleagues and friends, CliqDrop makes the process fast, private, and reliable.

1. Instant Cross-Device Sharing

Share content between mobile, desktop, laptop, office PC, or home PC ‚Äî anywhere Cliq or a browser is available. No cables, no email, no sync needed.

2. Share with Anyone (Not Only Cliq Users)

Users can retrieve data internally, externally, without contacts, or even without using Cliq (via website). All they need is the 6-digit code.

3. Secure and Temporary Storage

Only the unique 6-digit code can access data. No permanent storage. Auto-expiry and one-time-view options included.

4. Lightweight, Fast & Reliable

Upload once ‚Äî accessible instantly across all platforms. Works fast for both individuals and teams.

5. For Both Personal & Team Use

Perfect for moving files, sharing notes, sending documents, or transferring data privately.
